## How to Use

*   **Choose Your Habits:** Write down the habits you want to build in the "Habit / Goal" column.
*   **Track Daily:** Mark a □ in the corresponding day's column each time you complete a habit.
*   **Start Anytime:** This tracker has no dates, so begin on any day that works for you.
*   **Review & Reflect:** Use the reflection questions to understand your progress and stay motivated.
