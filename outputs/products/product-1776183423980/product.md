# Goal Tracker & Action Planner

Simple tools for better productivity

---

## How to Use

*   **Define Your Goals:** Clearly write down what you aim to achieve in the "My Goals" section.
*   **Break Down Actions:** For each goal, list the specific, actionable steps you need to take in the "Action Plan" section.
*   **Track Progress Daily:** Use the checkboxes to mark off completed tasks and habits each day.
*   **Review Weekly:** At the end of each week, reflect on your progress and challenges using the prompts provided.
*   **Stay Motivated:** Use the footer quote to keep your spirits high and focus on your achievements.
