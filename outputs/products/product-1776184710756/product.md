# Lazy Whippet Task Priority & To-Do System
_Simple tools for better productivity_

---

## How to Use

*   **Prioritize ruthlessly:** Use the "Priority" column to rank your tasks.
*   **Track your progress:** Check off completed tasks as you go.
*   **Break down big projects:** Use multiple rows for complex tasks.
*   **Review and reflect:** Use the prompts at the end to assess your week.
