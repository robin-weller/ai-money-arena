# 30-Day Habit Tracker
*Simple tools for better productivity*

## How to Use

*   **Choose Your Habits:** In the "Habit/Goal" column, write down the 3-5 habits you want to track for the next 30 days.
*   **Fill in the Dates:** At the top of each day's column (M, T, W, etc.), write the actual date.
*   **Track Daily:** Each day, check the box for each habit you successfully completed.
*   **Review Weekly:** Use the "Weekly Review" section to reflect on your progress.
*   **Reflect Monthly:** At the end of the 30 days, use the "30-Day Reflection" prompts to assess your journey.
