# ADHD-Friendly Daily Planner

Simple tools for better productivity

## How to Use

*   **Start with Intention:** Each morning, quickly jot down your top 3 priorities and any scheduled appointments to set a clear direction for your day.
*   **Chunk Your Tasks:** Break down larger projects into smaller, manageable steps in the "Tasks to Conquer" section. This makes them feel less overwhelming.
*   **Build Momentum with Habits:** Use the habit checkboxes to build consistent, positive routines that support your well-being and productivity.
*   **Fuel Your Body:** Keep track of your hydration and meal times to ensure you're energized throughout the day.
*   **Reflect & Recharge:** At the end of your day, take a few moments to review what went well, what was challenging, and what you can carry forward to tomorrow.
