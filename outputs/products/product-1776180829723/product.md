# ADHD-Friendly Daily Planner

Simple tools for better productivity

## How to Use

*   **Start your day with intention:** Before diving into tasks, fill in your top priorities and appointments.
*   **Break it down:** Use the "Tasks" section to list small, actionable steps for larger projects.
*   **Build positive habits:** Track your daily habits with the checkboxes to create consistency.
*   **Stay hydrated and energized:** Remember to log your water intake and meal times.
*   **Reflect and adjust:** At the end of the day, take a moment to review your progress and plan for tomorrow.
